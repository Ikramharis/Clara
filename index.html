Consequence matrix

What a violation actually costs

Severity determines the tolerance limit. A DRF (Disciplinary Record Form) always deducts KPI, on top of the record itself.

High Severity

Zero tolerance

PDPA breaches, credential sharing, unauthorised system changes. No warning tier — straight to dismissal.

Occurrence 1 → Dismissal
Medium Severity

One-strike tolerance

Personal devices, unattended documents, unapproved software. First occurrence issues a DRF and a KPI deduction.

Occurrence 1 → DRF · Occurrence 2 → Dismissal
Low Severity

Two-strike tolerance

Unapproved websites, unlocked unattended screens. More room to course-correct before dismissal.

1 → DRF · 2 → DRF 2 · 3 → Dismissal
